WHAT IS A COLD BREW

The Coffee Point Coffee uses cold brew to make all its coffees, unlike most
other coffees that are made using espressos.
Cold brew is made by steeping coarsely ground coffee in cold water
for 16-36 hours, resulting in a coffee which is smoother, less acidic
and has higher caffeine content.
